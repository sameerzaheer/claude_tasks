🤖 AI & LLMs

OpenAI launched Presence (July 22, 2026), an enterprise platform for deploying trusted AI voice and chat agents with built-in guardrails and a Codex-powered feedback loop; on OpenAI's own support line it already resolves 75% of inbound calls without human help. (https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-presence/)

Microsoft and Mistral AI expanded their strategic partnership (announced July 21, 2026) via a multibillion-dollar deal bringing thousands of Nvidia Vera Rubin GPUs to European data centers and Mistral's models into Azure, Foundry and Copilot Studio, including fully offline "sovereign" deployments for regulated industries. (https://news.microsoft.com/source/2026/07/21/microsoft-and-mistral-expand-strategic-partnership-to-give-enterprises-and-regulated-industries-frontier-ai-they-can-control/)

🏗️ AI in AEC, Construction & Engineering

University of Miami researchers unveiled CivilBot, an AI tool that converts plain-language structural descriptions directly into structural-analysis model code, cutting modeling time from days to hours — it generated a full model of Miami's Freedom Tower in about five minutes. (https://techxplore.com/news/2026-07-civilbot-faster.html)

McKinsey's new report "How agentic AI is transforming the AEC industry" (published July 15, 2026) found early adopters seeing real productivity gains in design, modeling and feasibility work, and urged firms to prioritize 3-5 high-value workflows rather than chase AI broadly. (https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/engineering-construction-and-building-materials/our-insights/how-ai-is-reshaping-the-future-of-the-aec-industry)
