🤖 AI & LLMs

Apple unveiled a rebuilt Siri at WWDC on June 8 powered by a custom 1.2-trillion-parameter Google Gemini model, with iOS 27 also letting users swap in ChatGPT or Anthropic's Claude as their AI extension of choice. (https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-june-8-2026)

The Pentagon confirmed it is evaluating OpenAI and Google models as potential replacements for Anthropic's Claude in classified government AI systems, reflecting growing multi-vendor competition for federal AI contracts. (https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-june-8-2026)
