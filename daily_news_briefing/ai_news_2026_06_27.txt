🤖 AI & LLMs

Google launched Gemini 2.5 Pro with Deep Think reasoning mode on June 22, 2026, setting a new benchmark record (GPQA Diamond 82.4%) with a 2M-token context window, making it the top publicly available model for math and science reasoning. (https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/gemini/gemini-2-5-deep-think/)

Google, Microsoft, GitHub, NVIDIA, Salesforce, Snowflake, and Hugging Face jointly released the Agentic Resource Discovery (ARD) open specification, enabling AI agents to autonomously find and connect with tools, APIs, and other agents at runtime — a foundational standard for multi-agent AI ecosystems. (https://www.marketingprofs.com/opinions/2026/55130/ai-update-june-26-2026-ai-news-and-views-from-the-past-week)

📋 AI in Procurement & Proposal Writing

The White House signed an executive order on "Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security" on June 26, 2026, streamlining AI acquisition processes for federal agencies and removing regulatory barriers to deploying frontier AI in government procurement. (https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/06/promoting-advanced-artificial-intelligence-innovation-and-security/)

The National Law Review published guidance warning that proposals not structured for AI-assisted evaluation now risk being filtered out before a human reviewer engages, as federal agencies increasingly deploy AI screening tools — forcing contractors to adopt AI-readable RFP formatting.
