Daily AI News Briefing — July 13, 2026

🤖 AI & LLMs

Mistral AI released Leanstral 1.5 (July 2, 2026), an open-weight, Apache-2.0-licensed Lean 4 model for formal software verification that solved 587 of 672 PutnamBench problems and caught five previously unknown bugs while scanning 57 open-source repositories. (https://mistral.ai/news/leanstral-1-5/)

OpenAI launched GPT-Live (July 8, 2026), a full-duplex voice model family that listens and speaks simultaneously and quietly delegates complex reasoning to GPT-5.5 in the background, replacing the turn-based Advanced Voice Mode. (https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-live/)

🚀 Startups

Jeff Bezos's industrial-AI startup Prometheus raised a $12 billion Series B (announced June 11, 2026) at a $41 billion valuation, backed by JPMorgan, BlackRock and Goldman Sachs, to build an "artificial general engineer" that automates design and manufacturing of physical systems from jet engines to drug compounds. (https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/11/jeff-bezoss-prometheus-raises-12b-to-build-an-artificial-general-engineer-for-the-physical-world/)

Patronus AI raised a $50 million round (announced June 25, 2026) to build simulated "digital worlds" that stress-test AI agents before they're deployed into production. (https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/25/patronus-ai-lands-50m-to-build-digital-worlds-that-stress-test-ai-agents/)
