(https://som.yale.edu/story/2026/som-alum-raises-6-million-ai-construction-startup)

📋 AI in Procurement & Proposal Writing

Federal agencies including GSA and the Pentagon are already using AI tools to evaluate contractor proposals, and teams that don't adapt their writing for AI evaluators risk falling behind in federal bids. (https://federalnewsnetwork.com/commentary/2026/04/federal-agencies-are-using-ai-to-evaluate-proposals-is-your-team-ready/)

The GAO reviewed AI acquisitions at DOD, DHS, GSA, and VA, finding that agencies do not systematically share lessons learned from AI procurements — a gap slowing the federal government's ability to scale effective AI adoption. (https://www.gao.gov/products/gao-26-107859)
