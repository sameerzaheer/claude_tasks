Microsoft launched MAI-Code-1-Flash at Build 2026, its first in-house coding model trained entirely without OpenAI data, now rolling out to GitHub Copilot users with a 16-point SWE-Bench Pro lead over Claude Haiku 4.5 and 60% fewer tokens needed per task. (https://microsoft.ai/news/introducingmai-code-1-flash/)
